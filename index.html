+ How this works

Demographic sliders do not add a flat citywide swing. Each slider changes precincts according to how exposed they are to that factor.

effect = coefficient x slider x precinct exposure

Direct candidate shifts are applied at each precinct-candidate row before candidate shares are normalized inside that precinct. Citywide totals are calculated afterward.
